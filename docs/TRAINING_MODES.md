# Training Modes

The scaffold includes a tiny one-step distillation smoke. Corridor, exemplar,
and two-cycle training modes will move from qrwkv-xla after artifact contracts
are stable.

Dense Tome targets can be loaded through the Student artifact layer for NumPy
smoke training. Sparse top-k loss support is available for future compressed
teacher-target adapters once those payloads are emitted by upstream artifacts.
